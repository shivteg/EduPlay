          About EduPlay

          EduPlay turns educational practice into an exciting, repeatable adventure with colorful worlds, gentle progression, and rewarding feedback.

          Full credit: created and designed by Shivteg Singh, a young coder with a passion for playful learning experiences.

          How EduPlay works

          • Pick a world and learn through progressively harder levels.
          • Answer quickly to earn XP, coins, and streaks.
          • Spend coins in the shop and unlock the next world after completing 10 levels.
          • Use the music and sound controls to tailor your learning mood.

          Designed and built by Shivteg Singh as a premium educational game experience with semantic HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript.
