        About EduPlay

        EduPlay turns educational practice into an exciting, repeatable adventure with colorful worlds, gentle progression, and rewarding feedback.

        Full credit: created and designed by Shivteg Singh, a young coder with a passion for playful learning experiences.

        How EduPlay works

        • Gameplay: Pick a level from the map path and start your quest. Answer 10 math questions per level.
        • Progressive Levels: Advance through 10 math themes (Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, Fractions, Time, Shapes, Patterns, Word Problems, and the Boss Battle).
        • Scoring & Multipliers: You gain points for correct answers. Earn consecutive answers correct to build a Combo Streak multiplier. Quick answers receive a speed time-bonus! Wrong answers deduct points.
        • Economy & Shop: Correct answers yield Coins. Shop at the rewards store to unlock special boosters (Hint, Time, Shield, Skip) or cosmetic Avatar Frames & Titles.
        • Wardrobe: Equip owned frames and titles in the "My Wardrobe" section of the dashboard to customize your profile!
        • Lucky Spin: Take a free spin on the Lucky Wheel once per session to win bonus coins or boosters.
        • Narration: Click the voice narration button (🔊) in the header to read questions out loud.
